ca.uqac.lif.cep.Connector.ConnectionException Class Reference

Detailed Description

Exception thrown when a problem occurs when connecting the output pipe of a processor to the input pipe of another one.

This exception is more specific than a ConnectorException, since it is related to two particular pipes of two processor instances.
