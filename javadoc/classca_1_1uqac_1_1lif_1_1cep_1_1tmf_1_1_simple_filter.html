Detailed Description

A simple filter that lets input events out by evaluating a condition on them; if the condition evaluates to true, the event is output, otherwise it is discarded.

There also exists a more generic type of filter: Filter, whose filtering decision is based on a trace of Booleans instead of a stateless condition.
